DRIZZLED Icing

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar
  • dash of vanilla
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey

STEPS

  1. Combine the confectioners' sugar and vanilla, then gradually add the milk, stirring vigorously to eliminate any lumps. Stir in the honey, aim for a thick, smooth consistency.
